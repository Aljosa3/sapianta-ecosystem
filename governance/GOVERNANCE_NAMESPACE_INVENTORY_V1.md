# 1. PURPOSE

This artifact inventories the current constitutional governance
namespaces for deterministic human and evidence review.

The inventory is constitutional metadata, not runtime intelligence.

---

# 2. NAMESPACE INVENTORY

## `governance/evolution/`

Purpose: evolution governance, EPP foundation, bounded refinement,
anti-overengineering, expansion criteria, replay requirements,
promotion requirements, and rollback guarantees.

Governance role: keeps future evolution proposal-first,
approval-gated, replay-aware, rollback-aware, and bounded.

Replay relevance: defines replay requirements before evolution may
become promotable.

Stabilization relevance: preserves refinement before expansion and
prevents stabilization findings from becoming architecture explosion.

Constitutional relevance: establishes governance-first evolution
constraints.

## `governance/constitutional/`

Purpose: constitutional governance foundations across GEP, replay
evidence, and promotion governance.

Governance role: records constitutional boundaries, authority models,
evidence requirements, and promotion constraints.

Replay relevance: contains canonical replay evidence and lineage
contracts.

Stabilization relevance: prevents operational discoveries from becoming
implicit runtime authority.

Constitutional relevance: primary constitutional governance namespace.

## `governance/constitutional/gep/`

Purpose: Governance Evolution Protocol foundation.

Governance role: defines governance mutation rules, authority
hierarchy, approval/evidence model, and LLM non-authority.

Replay relevance: governance evolution requires replay and lineage
evidence before activation.

Stabilization relevance: cognition pressure and stabilization findings
remain bounded by constitutional authority.

Constitutional relevance: freezes governance evolution boundaries
before adaptive governance runtime.

## `governance/constitutional/replay/`

Purpose: governance replay evidence schema, identity model, lineage
contract, manifest specification, rollback evidence, and promotion
evidence.

Governance role: makes replay evidence canonical before replay-driven
authority exists.

Replay relevance: primary replay evidence foundation.

Stabilization relevance: anchors replay continuity and chain integrity
hardening.

Constitutional relevance: freezes replay evidence semantics before
adaptive replay authority.

## `governance/constitutional/promotion/`

Purpose: constitutional promotion gate model, promotion classes,
approval hierarchy, replay requirements, rollback guarantees, and
quarantine semantics.

Governance role: prevents implicit promotion authority and
self-promotion.

Replay relevance: promotion without replay evidence is invalid.

Stabilization relevance: separates operational hardening from promotion
authority.

Constitutional relevance: freezes promotion semantics before promotion
engines.

## `governance/operational/`

Purpose: operational stabilization evidence, replay failure-mode notes,
fail-closed expectations, operational evidence indexes, and semantic
hardening records.

Governance role: preserves stabilization-era evidence without creating
runtime authority.

Replay relevance: records replay continuity, replay chain integrity,
and fail-closed replay expectations.

Stabilization relevance: primary namespace for stabilization-era
governance evidence.

Constitutional relevance: documents non-activation and bounded
operational evidence continuity.
