Status: NON-EXECUTING COGNITION VISIBILITY RULES
Layer: Governance Discovery

---

# 1. RULE PURPOSE

This artifact defines the non-executing boundary for cognition visibility.

Cognition visibility is constitutional evidence, not cognition runtime.

---

# 2. NON-EXECUTING RULES

Cognition visibility may:

- identify replay-safe cognition references;
- expose cognition lineage;
- describe ancestry continuity;
- certify non-activation boundaries;
- support future bounded review.

Cognition visibility may not:

- execute cognition;
- plan actions;
- orchestrate runtime behavior;
- trigger autonomous reasoning;
- mutate cognition references;
- create runtime semantic memory.

---

# 3. AUTHORITY BOUNDARY

Visibility does not grant authority.

Lineage does not grant execution.

Replay evidence does not grant cognition activation.

Continuity does not grant planning authority.
