# SEMANTIC_PROGRESSION_BOUNDARIES_V1
Status: SEMANTIC PROGRESSION BOUNDARIES
Layer: Governance Discovery
Principle: Semantic Continuity Without Semantic Autonomy

---

# 1. PURPOSE

This artifact defines constitutional boundaries for semantic
continuity, goal refinement, intent inheritance, milestone progression,
and semantic lineage continuity.

---

# 2. SEMANTIC CONTINUITY BOUNDARY

Semantic continuity preserves meaning across governance artifacts,
goals, intents, and milestones.

It does not activate cognition runtime.

---

# 3. GOAL REFINEMENT BOUNDARY

Goal refinement may clarify or narrow a goal while preserving identity.

It may not replace the goal secretly or drift semantically without
evidence.

---

# 4. INTENT INHERITANCE BOUNDARY

Intent inheritance must be explicit, replay-safe, bounded, and
governance-visible.

It may not be inferred through hidden semantic state.

---

# 5. MILESTONE PROGRESSION BOUNDARY

Milestone progression is a semantic lineage movement, not execution
orchestration.

Milestones may document readiness, closure, or next steps, but they do
not execute.

---

# 6. SEMANTIC LINEAGE BOUNDARY

Semantic lineage must remain deterministic and fail-closed on ambiguity.

Lineage does not authorize autonomous mutation.

---

# 7. PRESERVED DISCIPLINE

These boundaries preserve:

- replay-safe continuity;
- bounded refinement discipline;
- stabilization-before-semantic-amplification philosophy;
- anti-overengineering discipline;
- governance-constrained interpretation.

---

# 8. PROHIBITIONS

This artifact prohibits:

- semantic autonomy;
- unrestricted semantic evolution;
- hidden cognition activation;
- adaptive semantic mutation;
- semantic planning engines;
- semantic inference engines;
- autonomous goal execution.
