# Replay Baseline Freeze V1

Status: replay baseline freeze.

## Frozen Replay Definition

Replay is the deterministic evidence substrate that preserves operational and governance-visible history.

Replay is frozen as:

- constitutional source of truth
- canonical lineage substrate
- authoritative continuity layer
- replay-visible evidence spine

## Frozen Replay Terms

The following replay terms are frozen as canonical:

- replay
- lineage
- continuity
- replay visibility
- replay integrity
- replay isolation

## Frozen Replay Guarantees

Replay guarantees:

- deterministic lineage
- append-only evidence
- ordered continuity
- unique identity where applicable
- fail-closed ambiguity handling
- isolation between replay scopes

## Prohibited Replay Drift

Future evolution must not introduce:

- parallel replay definitions
- hidden replay mutation
- adaptive replay interpretation
- autonomous replay repair
- replay-as-memory semantics
- replay-as-authority semantics

## Replay Freeze Rule

Any future continuity model must preserve replay centrality and explain how it remains subordinate to replay-visible lineage.
