# 2. COMPLEXITY FINDINGS

## Minimality

Finding: PASS.

milestone, progression boundaries, replay-safe continuity, and boundary
guarantees.

introduced.

## Determinism

Finding: PASS.

The artifacts consistently require deterministic references,
replay-safe lineage, evidence-visible relationships, and fail-closed
ambiguity handling.

## Boundedness

Finding: PASS.

The artifacts preserve bounded refinement and repeatedly prohibit
autonomous mutation, adaptive rewriting, hidden replacement, and
execution authority.

## Governance Orientation

Finding: PASS.

The substrate remains documentation/evidence-only and
governance-constrained.

## Replay Safety

Finding: PASS.

Semantic continuity is tied to concrete references, append-only
progression visibility, and fail-closed ambiguity behavior.
