# 1. PURPOSE

This artifact defines visibility expectations for bounded refinement,
anti-overengineering boundaries, replay-safe operational continuity,
fail-closed semantics, and constitutional stabilization invariants.

---

# 2. BOUNDED REFINEMENT VISIBILITY

Bounded refinement discipline SHOULD remain visible through governance
artifacts that document semantic correction, deterministic hardening,
and continuity preservation before architecture expansion.

---

# 3. ANTI-OVERENGINEERING BOUNDARIES

Anti-overengineering boundaries SHOULD remain visible through
architecture expansion criteria, complexity risk models, freeze
manifests, and non-activation certifications.

---

# 4. REPLAY-SAFE OPERATIONAL CONTINUITY

Replay-safe operational continuity SHOULD remain visible through replay
evidence, governed returns, replay identity continuity, replay chain
integrity, and session continuity artifacts.

---

# 5. FAIL-CLOSED SEMANTICS

Fail-closed semantics SHOULD remain visible wherever ambiguity,
malformed evidence, lineage corruption, inconsistent ordering, or
unverifiable continuity may occur.

---

# 6. CONSTITUTIONAL STABILIZATION INVARIANTS

Stabilization invariants:

- stabilization precedes expansion;
- refinement precedes redesign;
- replay safety precedes mutation authority;
- governance evidence precedes activation;
- ambiguous continuity fails closed.

---

# 7. PRESERVATION STATEMENT

This artifact preserves stabilization-before-expansion philosophy,
bounded refinement philosophy, and replay-safe governance continuity.

---

monitoring, autonomous repair, governance execution runtime, semantic
