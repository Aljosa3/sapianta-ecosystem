Status: GOVERNANCE ARTIFACT CLASSIFICATION INVENTORY
Layer: Governance Discovery
Principle: Deterministic Classification Without Adaptive Ranking

---

# 1. PURPOSE

This artifact defines canonical governance artifact categories for the
current inventory snapshot.

Classification is descriptive. It does not rank evidence, infer
meaning, route governance, or activate runtime behavior.

---

# 2. ARTIFACT CATEGORIES

## FOUNDATION

Purpose: establishes constitutional, governance, or operational base
semantics.

Governance role: defines the baseline boundary for later artifacts.

Replay relationship: may define replay prerequisites or replay-safe
conditions.

Operational relationship: constrains how operation may be interpreted.

Stabilization relationship: provides the stable ground for later
stabilization findings.

## REPLAY

Purpose: defines replay evidence, replay identity, replay lineage, or
replay integrity semantics.

Governance role: preserves deterministic evidence and verification
expectations.

Replay relationship: primary category for replay-safe traceability.

Operational relationship: supports operator-visible replay trust.

Stabilization relationship: records replay hardening and continuity
semantics.

## PROMOTION

Purpose: defines promotion eligibility, approval, replay, rollback, and
quarantine requirements.

Governance role: prevents implicit promotion and self-promotion.

Replay relationship: promotion requires replay evidence.

Operational relationship: separates operational hardening from
promotion authority.

Stabilization relationship: prevents stabilization improvements from
becoming activation.

## OPERATIONAL

Purpose: records operational runtime, observability, or stabilization
evidence.

Governance role: preserves operational evidence without expanding
authority.

Replay relationship: often records replay continuity and runtime
evidence.

Operational relationship: primary category for observed behavior.

Stabilization relationship: records controlled stabilization outcomes.

## STABILIZATION

Purpose: records discoveries, lessons, and bounded corrections surfaced
during controlled stabilization.

Governance role: keeps learning bounded and evidence-visible.

Replay relationship: captures replay continuity and replay integrity
discoveries.

Operational relationship: documents operational semantics.

Stabilization relationship: primary stabilization category.

## BOUNDARY_PRESERVATION

Purpose: certifies that artifacts did not introduce runtime authority,
orchestration, mutation, or architecture expansion.

Governance role: protects freeze boundaries.

Replay relationship: preserves replay-safe non-mutation semantics.

Operational relationship: prevents operational artifacts from becoming
runtime behavior.

Stabilization relationship: preserves stabilization-before-expansion.

## SEMANTIC_HARDENING

Purpose: documents bounded correction of proven semantic weakness.

Governance role: prefers refinement before redesign.

Replay relationship: strengthens deterministic replay interpretation.

Operational relationship: improves operator trust without expansion.

Stabilization relationship: converts findings into bounded correction.

## INTERPRETATION_GUIDANCE

Purpose: helps humans read governance evidence without overinterpreting
passive artifacts.

Governance role: prevents governance drift and topology confusion.

Replay relationship: clarifies replay evidence as traceability.

Operational relationship: clarifies operational evidence as memory, not
runtime intelligence.

Stabilization relationship: preserves bounded interpretation of
stabilization evidence.

## INVENTORY

Purpose: records deterministic metadata about governance artifacts and
namespaces.

Governance role: preserves discoverability.

Replay relationship: preserves replay-safe references.

Operational relationship: helps locate operational evidence.

Stabilization relationship: prevents stabilization artifact loss.

## TOPOLOGY

Purpose: maps governance namespaces and relationships.

Governance role: prevents isolated governance islands.

Replay relationship: shows replay governance placement.

Operational relationship: shows operational evidence placement.

Stabilization relationship: connects stabilization to evolution and
constitutional foundations.

## LINEAGE

Purpose: records governance progression and artifact ancestry.

Governance role: preserves continuity.

Replay relationship: supports replay-safe governance progression.

Operational relationship: connects observed behavior to later evidence.

Stabilization relationship: records why hardening milestones exist.
