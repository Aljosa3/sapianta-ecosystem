# AUTHORITY_IDENTITY_BOUNDARIES_V1
Status: AUTHORITY IDENTITY BOUNDARIES
Layer: Runtime Boundary Evidence

---

# 1. AUTHORITY IDENTITY

Authority identity is constitutionally scoped, immutable during runtime,
and non-transferable implicitly.

Bounded runtime identity carries no governance authority and no execution
authority.

---

# 2. DRIFT PREVENTION

Authority continuity validation blocks:

- implicit authority continuation;
- identity escalation attempts;
- authority inheritance ambiguity;
- execution authority activation;
- governance authority persistence.

---

# 3. FAIL-CLOSED RULE

Any authority drift or authority inheritance ambiguity fails closed and
must remain replay-visible.
