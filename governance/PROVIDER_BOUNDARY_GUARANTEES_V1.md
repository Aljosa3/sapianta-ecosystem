# Provider Boundary Guarantees V1

Status: constitutional provider boundary guarantees.

## Certified Guarantees

Provider cannot execute.

Provider cannot authorize.

Provider cannot govern.

Provider cannot dispatch.

Provider cannot mutate replay.

Provider cannot mutate memory.

Provider remains proposal-only.

## Boundary Rule

Provider output may become proposal evidence.

Provider output may not become:

- execution authority
- governance authority
- authorization authority
- replay authority
- memory authority
- worker authority

## AiGOL Control

AiGOL remains responsible for:

- validation
- governance evaluation
- replay recording
- fail-closed enforcement
- authority separation

Provider remains external proposal producer only.
