# 2. NAMESPACE CLASSIFICATION

## EVOLUTION

Governance purpose: proposal-first evolution governance, scope locking,
bounded refinement, and anti-overengineering discipline.

Architectural role: defines how future evolution may be proposed before
activation.

Replay relationship: requires replay evidence before evolution may be
promotable.

Lineage relationship: preserves proposal, promotion, and rollback
lineage.

Operational relationship: prevents operational discoveries from becoming
unbounded expansion.

## CONSTITUTIONAL

Governance purpose: immutable and approval-gated constitutional
boundaries.

Architectural role: establishes authority hierarchy and mutation
constraints.

Replay relationship: requires replay visibility for governance changes.

Lineage relationship: preserves constitutional ancestry.

Operational relationship: bounds operational runtime interpretation.

## REPLAY

Governance purpose: canonical replay evidence, identity, manifest, and
lineage semantics.

Architectural role: defines replay evidence before replay-driven
authority exists.

Replay relationship: primary namespace for replay evidence contracts.

Lineage relationship: requires append-only traceability.

Operational relationship: supports replay stabilization evidence.

## PROMOTION

Governance purpose: approval-gated promotion semantics and rollback
requirements.

Architectural role: separates eligibility from execution authority.

Replay relationship: promotion without replay evidence is invalid.

Lineage relationship: promotion and rollback lineage remain visible.

Operational relationship: prevents hardening from implying promotion.

## OPERATIONAL

Governance purpose: stabilization evidence and operational semantics
notes.

Architectural role: records runtime observations without creating
runtime authority.

Replay relationship: captures replay discoveries and fail-closed
expectations.

Lineage relationship: preserves why refinements occurred.

Operational relationship: primary evidence namespace for stabilization
findings.

## STABILIZATION

Governance purpose: preserve evidence from controlled stabilization
windows.

Architectural role: supports refinement before expansion.

Replay relationship: captures replay continuity and chain integrity
discoveries.

Lineage relationship: links observed issues to bounded corrections.

Operational relationship: prevents repeated observations from drifting
into implicit autonomy.

## BOUNDARY_PRESERVATION

Governance purpose: certify non-activation and scope preservation.

Architectural role: prevents documentation milestones from becoming
runtime expansion.

Replay relationship: preserves replay-safe boundaries.

Lineage relationship: documents non-mutating evidence creation.

Operational relationship: protects operational freeze discipline.

## SEMANTIC_HARDENING

Governance purpose: document bounded correction of proven semantic
weakness.

Architectural role: refines meaning without architecture redesign.

Replay relationship: supports deterministic replay interpretation.

Lineage relationship: links discovery to correction.

Operational relationship: preserves stabilization-first refinement.
