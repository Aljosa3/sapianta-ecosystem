# COGNITION_REPLAY_TRANSITIONS_V1
Status: COGNITION REPLAY TRANSITION SEMANTICS
Layer: Governance Discovery

---

# 1. TRANSITION PURPOSE

Cognition replay transitions describe deterministic visibility movement
between cognition-adjacent governance references.

They are continuity markers, not runtime cognition transitions.

---

# 2. TRANSITION EXAMPLE

Bounded lineage progression may be represented as:

stabilization continuity
-> semantic continuity
-> reasoning continuity
-> cognition boundary
-> cognition replay visibility

This progression remains passive, governance-bounded, and replay-safe.

---

# 3. TRANSITION RULES

Cognition replay transitions must:

- preserve deterministic ordering;
- preserve replay-safe lineage;
- preserve governance visibility;
- remain append-only;
- fail closed on ambiguity.

---

# 4. PROHIBITIONS

Cognition replay transitions do not activate cognition runtime, planning
runtime, orchestration, autonomous reasoning, or adaptive cognition.
