# INTENT_CONTINUITY_SEMANTICS_V1
Status: INTENT CONTINUITY SEMANTICS
Layer: Governance Discovery
Principle: Bounded Semantic Direction Without Autonomous Planning

---

# 1. PURPOSE

This artifact defines intent persistence semantics, cross-session intent
continuity, bounded semantic inheritance, replay-safe intent
traceability, and deterministic continuity semantics.

---

# 2. INTENT PERSISTENCE

Intent persistence means a semantic direction can remain visible across
sessions, milestones, or refinement artifacts.

Intent persistence is descriptive and bounded.

---

# 3. CROSS-SESSION INTENT CONTINUITY

Cross-session intent continuity SHOULD preserve semantic direction
through replay-safe references and deterministic lineage.

It does not create runtime memory, adaptive reasoning, or autonomous
planning.

---

# 4. BOUNDED SEMANTIC INHERITANCE

Intent may inherit from prior governance, stabilization, or semantic
continuity artifacts only through explicit references.

Hidden inheritance is prohibited.

---

# 5. REPLAY-SAFE INTENT TRACEABILITY

Intent traceability SHOULD remain:

- deterministic;
- replay-safe;
- governance-visible;
- human-readable;
- fail-closed on ambiguity.

---

# 6. INTENT CONTINUITY RULE

Intent continuity preserves semantic direction without autonomous
planning.

Intent continuity may describe what remains semantically consistent, but
it may not authorize execution.

---

# 7. PROHIBITIONS

Intent continuity MUST NOT support:

- hidden intent rewriting;
- adaptive semantic drift;
- autonomous intent mutation;
- semantic planning runtime;
- cognition runtime;
- agentic autonomy.

---

# 8. ARCHITECTURAL STATUS

This artifact adds no cognition runtime, orchestration, autonomous
planning, adaptive reasoning, semantic mutation authority, runtime goal
execution, or semantic inference engine.
