# COGNITION_REPLAY_SNAPSHOTS_V1
Status: COGNITION REPLAY SNAPSHOT SEMANTICS
Layer: Governance Discovery

---

# 1. SNAPSHOT PURPOSE

Cognition replay snapshots provide replay-visible checkpoints for
cognition-adjacent continuity evidence.

Snapshots are passive evidence references.

Snapshots are not runtime cognition memory.

---

# 2. SNAPSHOT REQUIREMENTS

Cognition replay snapshots must remain:

- deterministic;
- replay-safe;
- append-only;
- governance-bounded;
- non-executing;
- fail-closed on ambiguity.

---

# 3. SNAPSHOT CONTENT

A cognition replay snapshot may reference:

- cognition boundary state;
- reasoning continuity ancestry;
- semantic continuity ancestry;
- replay visibility guarantees;
- fail-closed ambiguity expectations.

---

# 4. SNAPSHOT PROHIBITIONS

Cognition replay snapshots may not perform cognition, plan actions,
mutate governance, trigger orchestration, or preserve hidden runtime
cognition memory.
