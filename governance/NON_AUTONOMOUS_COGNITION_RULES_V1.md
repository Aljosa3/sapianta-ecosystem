# NON_AUTONOMOUS_COGNITION_RULES_V1
Status: NON-AUTONOMOUS COGNITION RULES
Layer: Governance Discovery
Principle: Cognition Boundaries Must Preserve Non-Autonomy

---

# 1. PURPOSE

This artifact defines non-autonomous cognition rules.

---

# 2. RULES

Cognition boundaries may constrain future cognition.

Cognition boundaries may define what cognition cannot do.

Cognition boundaries may define fail-closed ambiguity handling.

Cognition boundaries may remain replay-visible.

Cognition boundaries may not execute.

Cognition boundaries may not plan.

Cognition boundaries may not self-authorize.

Cognition boundaries may not mutate themselves.

Cognition boundaries may not coordinate runtime behavior.

---

# 3. PROHIBITIONS

This artifact prohibits:

- autonomous cognition;
- adaptive self-modifying cognition;
- recursive autonomous cognition;
- planning runtime activation;
- orchestration-driven cognition;
- hidden runtime cognition memory;
- agentic behavior.

---

# 4. CERTIFICATION STATEMENT

Bounded cognition remains non-autonomous unless a later governed
milestone explicitly authorizes and constrains a new capability. No such
authorization exists here.
