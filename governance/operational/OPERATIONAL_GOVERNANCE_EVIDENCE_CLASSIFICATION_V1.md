# OPERATIONAL_GOVERNANCE_EVIDENCE_CLASSIFICATION_V1
Status: GOVERNANCE EVIDENCE CLASSIFICATION
Layer: Operational Governance Evidence
Principle: Deterministic Classification Without Adaptive Interpretation

---

# 1. PURPOSE

This artifact defines canonical operational evidence classes for
stabilization-era governance notes, replay discoveries, semantic
hardening milestones, and fail-closed evidence.

---

# 2. EVIDENCE CLASSIFICATION MODEL

## REPLAY_CONTINUITY_REFINEMENT

Purpose: documents corrections to replay identity uniqueness,
monotonicity, and deterministic continuity.

Scope: replay identity semantics only.

Replay relevance: preserves replay identity traceability.

Lineage relevance: reduces ambiguity in lineage references.

Governance relevance: supports future governance evidence confidence.

## REPLAY_CHAIN_INTEGRITY

Purpose: documents replay chain integrity risks and validation
expectations.

Scope: gaps, duplicates, ordering corruption, ancestry corruption, and
append-only continuity.

Replay relevance: preserves deterministic replay chain interpretation.

Lineage relevance: strengthens ancestry and ordering confidence.

Governance relevance: supports fail-closed replay trust.

## FAIL_CLOSED_EXPECTATIONS

Purpose: documents deterministic refusal expectations under replay
ambiguity.

Scope: ambiguous continuity, malformed sequence, duplicate identity,
corrupted lineage, and unverifiable evidence.

Replay relevance: ensures replay uncertainty does not become partial
acceptance.

Lineage relevance: prevents lineage ambiguity from being treated as
valid.

Governance relevance: preserves fail-closed governance semantics.

## STABILIZATION_DISCOVERY

Purpose: documents operational issues discovered during controlled
stabilization.

Scope: observed runtime/replay semantics, operational ambiguity, and
bounded lessons.

Replay relevance: preserves discovery context for replay hardening.

Lineage relevance: records why later semantic corrections exist.

Governance relevance: keeps operational evidence auditable.

## SEMANTIC_HARDENING

Purpose: documents bounded correction of proven semantic weakness.

Scope: semantics, continuity, evidence clarity, and operator
observability.

Replay relevance: records refinement without replay architecture
redesign.

Lineage relevance: preserves continuity of operational learning.

Governance relevance: demonstrates refinement before expansion.

## BOUNDARY_PRESERVATION

Purpose: documents that a governance evidence milestone did not expand
runtime, replay, orchestration, or governance authority.

Scope: non-activation certification and scope preservation.

Replay relevance: protects replay-safe boundaries.

Lineage relevance: records that evidence creation did not mutate
operational lineage.

Governance relevance: preserves constitutional and operational freeze
boundaries.

---

# 3. CLASSIFICATION RULE

Evidence classification is descriptive only. It does not rank evidence,
infer meaning, activate behavior, or authorize runtime interpretation.
