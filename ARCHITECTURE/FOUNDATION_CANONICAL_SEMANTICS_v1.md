## Canonical Semantic Ownership

Topology semantics are owned by the ecosystem topology layer.

Authority semantics are owned by the repository authority and launcher authority layers.

Interaction semantics are owned by the repository interaction layers.

Lifecycle semantics are owned by the domain lifecycle layer.

Factory semantics are owned by the factory isolation and proposal flow layers.

Acceptance semantics are owned by the runtime acceptance gate layer.

Replay, lineage, identity, promotion, audit, inheritance, and integrity semantics are owned by the replay and governed artifact identity layers.

## Prohibited Reinterpretations

- Conflicting reinterpretations are prohibited.
- Duplicate semantic layers are prohibited.
- Hidden authority semantics are prohibited.
- Implicit activation semantics are prohibited.
- Runtime execution authority must not be inferred from documentation.
- Governance memory must not be interpreted as runtime governance activation.

## Canonical Architectural Meaning

The foundation defines SAPIANTA as a deterministic, governance-first, replay-safe, authority-explicit, multi-repository AI ecosystem.

The foundation remains dormant architecture unless future implementation is explicitly approved through governance review.
