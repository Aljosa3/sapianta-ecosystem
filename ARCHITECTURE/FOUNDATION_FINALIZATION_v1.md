# SAPIANTA Foundation Finalization v1

## Document Role

This document finalizes the governed ecosystem foundation as a stable architectural baseline.

It is documentation-only. It does not implement runtime behavior, orchestration, governance activation, enforcement, automation, or autonomous execution.

## Finalization Statement

The SAPIANTA governed ecosystem foundation is stabilized for Foundation Phase v1.

Future work must extend foundation semantics.

Future work must not redefine foundation semantics.

## Stabilized Semantics

Topology stability:
The meta root, governed runtime, domain repositories, factory, and governance memory boundaries are stable.

Authority stability:
Repository authority, mutation authority, import authority, launcher authority, and factory non-authority are stable.

Replay stability:
Replay lineage, replay propagation, deterministic replay identity, and replay-safe promotion expectations are stable.

Lineage stability:
Artifact identity, promotion continuity, audit continuity, artifact inheritance, and lineage integrity hardening are stable.

Lifecycle stability:
Domain lifecycle states and governance-gated transition expectations are stable.

## Interpretation Freeze

Foundation Phase v1 documents are canonical for interpreting SAPIANTA as a governed, deterministic, replay-safe, multi-repository AI ecosystem.

Conflicting reinterpretations are prohibited unless a future human-reviewed ADR explicitly supersedes this foundation.

## Future Evolution

Future ecosystem evolution must remain:
- deterministic
- replay-safe
- governance-first
- bounded
- lineage-preserving
- authority-explicit

Possible future evolution may include:
- additional domains
- additional governance layers
- federated orchestration
- regulated execution ecosystems

Current status:
- NOT IMPLEMENTED
- NOT ACTIVE
- NOT AUTHORIZED
