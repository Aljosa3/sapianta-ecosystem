# Governance Finalization Evidence

Status: constitutional governance finalization artifact.

## What Was Finalized

The constitutional governance subsystem is finalized as a canonical, evidence-backed specification and verification layer.

Finalized artifacts include:

- constitutional architecture specification;
- canonical layer model;
- constitutional invariants;
- governance enforcement hierarchy;
- governance lineage model;
- governance conformance system;
- deterministic conformance engine;
- conformance evidence and report;
- finalization manifest and milestone certification.

## Why It Is Considered Finalized

The subsystem is considered finalized because it now has:

- a canonical taxonomy for L0-L4 governance layers;
- preserved mapping to the historical safety authority model;
- documented invariant, replay, mutation, and lineage semantics;
- a deterministic conformance verifier;
- explicit evidence of current conformance status;
- known limitations recorded rather than hidden.

Finalized does not mean finished forever. It means the current constitutional meaning is stable, replay-safe, scoped, and auditable.

## Guarantees Now Established

The finalized subsystem guarantees that future contributors can identify:

- what Layer 0 means;
- what is immutable;
- what may evolve;
- what is replay-critical;
- what requires governance review;
- where enforcement exists;
- where enforcement is partial;
- where documentation-only governance must not be treated as runtime activation.

## Enforcement That Exists

The finalized evidence recognizes these enforcement surfaces:

- Layer 0 freeze manifest and checker;
- ArchitectureGuardian;
- MutationGuard;
- MutationValidator;
- PromotionGate;
- DevGovernanceGate;
- CCS certification;
- replay chain verification;
- domain constitutional review components;
- governance conformance engine.

## Intentionally Unresolved

The finalization preserves known limitations:

- installed hook enforcement does not fully match documented hook expectations;
- conformance verification is evidence-oriented, not formal proof;
- repository-wide constitutional enforcement remains distributed;
- some governance memory remains documentation-only by design;
- domain-specific constitutional guarantees remain scoped to their domain.

## Outside Finalized Scope

This finalization does not introduce:

- runtime behavior changes;
- governance redesign;
- autonomous mutation;
- broker/API execution;
- production execution;
- AI reasoning;
- automated remediation.
