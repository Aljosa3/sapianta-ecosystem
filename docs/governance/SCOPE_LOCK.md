# Scope Lock

Status: constitutional governance finalization artifact.

## Locked

The following semantics are locked by this finalization:

- constitutional architecture semantics;
- canonical L0-L4 layer meanings;
- invariant semantics;
- replay read-only semantics;
- enforcement hierarchy semantics;
- governance lineage semantics;
- distinction between mutation layers and safety authority layers;
- distinction between active development enforcement and dormant governance memory.

Locked means these meanings must not be silently reinterpreted. Future change requires explicit governance evidence.

## Controlled

The following may evolve through governed change:

- enforcement implementations;
- conformance rule coverage;
- governance reporting structure;
- evidence packaging;
- hook installation and verification;
- certification reporting;
- lineage indexing.

Controlled evolution must preserve constitutional meaning, replay safety, and known limitation visibility.

## Adaptive

The following may evolve only in bounded future layers:

- remediation proposals;
- future governance hardening;
- future optimization layers;
- future adaptive governance research;
- future conformance expansion.

Adaptive evolution may propose changes, but it may not mutate constitutional semantics or activate runtime governance without governed review.

## Outside Scope

This finalization does not lock:

- business product copy;
- UI presentation details;
- non-governance experiments;
- domain-specific feature implementation outside protected governance scopes.

These remain subject to ordinary repository rules unless they touch protected constitutional surfaces.
